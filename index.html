📋
HOW TO PLAY
  • Play the NYT Wordle every day — same puzzle for everyone
  • After finishing, tap Share and paste your result in the Submit Score tab
  • Scores are tracked per player, per day
  • No double submissions — one score per puzzle per player
🎯
SCORING
  • Your score is the number of guesses used (1–6)
  • Failed puzzle (X/6) counts as 7 points
  • Lower score = better — 1 guess is perfect, 6 is barely scraping by
  • Monthly rank is decided by total points, tiebroken by avg
🏆
MONTHLY WINNER
  • Player with the lowest total score wins the month
  • Tiebreaker: lowest average score per game
  • Monthly winner earns 1 season point
  • Winner gets bragging rights and eternal Hall of Fame glory
💰
